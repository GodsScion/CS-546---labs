Sai gadi pichi


Really sorry if you can't understand it. The story is a mix of Telugu and english. A book that I am working on.


Title chusi veediki emaina picha, enti e pichi title anukokandi, nenu na jan jigirini pilavadam ala pilusta. Kahani modalupete mundu, evari mano bhavalu debbatina kudadu ani, oka chinna formality.

Disclaimer: Ee kathaloni patralu kevalam kalpitalu matrame, Any resemblance to a real person or people in the story is pure coincidence (KAKAPOVACHU).

Mi anumanam nijamele, anni em kalpitalu kavu. Anyways lets begin. Cut cheste opening scene, e roju sayantram (almost night 6:00pm) nenu Inorbit mall lo food court lo kurchuni coffee tagutunta, Never mind Naku coffee alavatu ledu, edo tagutuna le butterscotch milkshake o chocolate milkshake o whatever. Dooram ninchi chudataniki oka abbai evarikosamo waiting la anipistadi (Ala chudakandi, coffee alavatu leni manushulu kuda untaru!).

Appude phone tesi tanaki text chesa "Ekkade? Inka enta sepu?" She replied "10 min, ippude mall enter avtuna".

Ala antundi ante ee pichidaniki inko 20 min patidi anukondi, leduledu min 30 min veskondi. E lopu sardaga na gurinchi chepta. Meeku title chodagane ardam ipoiuntadi, na peru Sai ani. Hi, nice to meet you. Purti peru ippude kadule, tarvata inka epudaina chepta (no guarantees). Na parents gurinchi chepali ante well accomplished General Manager father, over bhakti housewife mother. Valaki oka kanu oka kodukuni, so parledu bane premaga garabanga pencharu. Naku Infosys lo job. Na salary gurinchi adakandi, danini chuste siggu leni valaki kuda chepukodaniki siggu vestundi. Appatlo I’m single, ready to mingle.

Ila meeto matalo busy ga undaga sudden ga na shoulder meda oka tap, "OMG! She's finally here" ani venaki tirigi rose iva botunapudu realise aya, na anta Pushpam gadu apudu inka evadu undadu emo ani. Tap chesindi evaro some stranger, babu ki pakana unna chair kavalanta.

Jevvitam lo ___ (enno saro gurtu ledu) moham kotisinatu ga anipinchindi. Samyaniki ravalsina valu matram raru kani evarevaro animutyalu andaru vastuntaru.

Inkem chestam teeskondi ani anadam tappa. Ina manaki mudu chairlu kante ekkuva avasaram kuda ledule.

Sarlendi eelopu meeku emaina kavalante techukondi (Tea o, coffee o, whatever you prefer), nenu ikkade wait chestu mee chair ni kaapadtu untanu. Enti andi em techukovatledu? Nenu mimalni vadilesi ekadiki velanu lendi, ikkade unta, meeru kuda edokati techukoni naku company ivandi.

Techukunara? Nice. Chinnagane araginchandi, tondara em ledu, inka 20 min easy ga patiddi le tanu ravadaniki. Kani ipoina tarvata tanu raka munde manam marchipokunda table ni clean cheyanchali. Lekapote nenu raka munde order chesi tagutunav ra yadava ani godava modalupeti ekkada end chesiddo kuda telidu.


(My dear ladies, next 4 paras skip cheyandi. Aah ina meeru epudu ma mata vinarule.)

Ee aadavalu chala danger bayo, cinema steryotyping kaadu, personal experience. Valu godava padali ani decide ite aa devudu kuda vela takidiki bayapadalsinde. Elagite oka lioness dadicheyataniki sarina avakasam kosam kachukuni untado, veelu kuda sarina avaksam kosam opikga edurchustuntaru.

Lioness ina hunt chese mundu alochinchi logic to hunt chesidi emo kani, veelaki asalu logic ante ento kuda telidu emo, ekado start ayi ekado end iddi, enduku vadistunaro kuda telidu. Reasons elantivi ina avachu, apudu jarugutuna argument context ki sambandame undalsina avasaram ledu and valu provide chese arguments lo sense untadani guarantee kuda ledu.

Tanu ice cream adigite kasurukuni konapatleda? Prepare aypo she decided to fight and starts waiting, apudu em anaru. Darina potuna Benz car bagundi ani chusava? Andulo porpatuna ammai undaa?, prepare aypo, neeku gurtundadu. Pichipichiga matladutundi ani amani arichava? Inka nuvu prepare avalsina avasaram ledu (May god save you!). Em astralu (topics), enni enni sarlu vadutundo ni talrata bati untadi. Nuvu vodipoyav ani telsina e poratam agakapovachu, vala deggara una astralu ani minimum okasarina vadutaru. Nuvu inka bratiki unte twaraga recover avalani my prayers.

Puratana tavakalalo mariyu memory power lo competitions emaina unte ee adavalani pampandi baya. First, second, third vati tarvatayi kuda adavale untaru emo. Manalo mana mata bhaya, naku 2 gantala mundu tina breakfast ento kuda gurtundadu, veelu 2 samvacharalu munduyi kuda ela gurtupetukuntaro nakite ardam kavatledu.

Naku telusu meeru em anukuntunaru “Ina andaru ammailu ala enduku untaru le?” ani. Aa porapatu nenu kuda chesale. Then one fine day, gnanodayam indi. Aa roju gurinchi inkeppudaina chepta
